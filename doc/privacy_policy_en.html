Privacy Policy

Senar Gas Services Application

Introduction

At Senar App, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect the information we obtain through your use of the Senar App.

Information We Collect

Account Information

We collect information such as name, phone number, email address, password (encrypted), and profile picture (optional).

Location Data

We collect location data to determine customer location, track agents, identify the nearest agents to customers, display nearby stores, and improve delivery service. Location data is collected only when the app is in use, and for free agents, location can be tracked in the background only when the "Available" feature is activated for work.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve our services, process orders, communicate with you, and send important notifications.

Information Sharing

We may share your information with service providers, agents, and technicians to enable them to provide the requested service. We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes without your consent.

App Permissions

Senar App requests the following permissions:

  • Location: To determine your location, provide customized services, and track agents.
  • Notifications: To send updates about your orders and important notifications.

You can manage these permissions through your device settings at any time.
